Welcome to Lovely Nails & Spa


We are a privately owned family business operated by Charlotte Natives in the Weddington area, where you are greeted by warm smiles and a relaxed atmosphere. Our grade A salon is equipped to handle large parties as well as select services. We strive for 100% customer satisfaction and overall beauty and health wellness. We offer vast arrays of organic products as well as professional grade nail, skin, hair removal and eyelash products. A salon frequent by locals and where everyone is treated like family.

At Lovely Nails, your safety is our top priority. We use ULTRACLAVE AUTOMATIC STERILIZE (as seen at Dental office) to clean all of our instruments before each use. We also use a disposable set of tools for each new customer.

Cancellation Policy:

Refund Policy:
